# Day 6 - Advanced Analytics & Risk Metrics Summary

## Objective
Performed advanced financial analytics on mutual fund and investor datasets to evaluate risk, investor behavior, portfolio concentration, and provide personalized fund recommendations.

## Tasks Completed
- Calculated Historical Value at Risk (VaR) and Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR) for all mutual fund schemes.
- Computed Rolling 90-Day Sharpe Ratio and visualized risk-adjusted performance for the top five funds.
- Performed Investor Cohort Analysis based on first SIP investment year.
- Conducted SIP Continuity Analysis and identified at-risk investors using transaction gap analysis.
- Developed a Python-based mutual fund recommender using Sharpe Ratio and risk appetite.
- Measured portfolio concentration using the Herfindahl-Hirschman Index (HHI).
- Documented advanced business insights within the Jupyter Notebook.
