# Day 3 – Exploratory Data Analysis Summary

## Objectives

Perform exploratory data analysis on the cleaned mutual fund datasets and generate meaningful visualizations with business insights.

* Analyzed daily NAV trends across 40 mutual fund schemes using Plotly.
* Visualized AUM growth of major fund houses using Seaborn.
* Studied monthly SIP inflow trends and highlighted the highest inflow.

* Analyzed investor demographics through age, gender, and investment distributions.
* Explored geographic investment distribution by state and city tier.
* Visualized industry folio count growth.
* Computed correlation between selected mutual fund returns.
* Aggregated sector allocation across equity mutual funds.
* Documented insights for every visualization in the notebook.

Successfully completed the EDA notebook with interactive and static financial visualizations, along with business insights for each analysis.
